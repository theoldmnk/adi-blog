why you should build things

— march 13, 2025

the internet is such a great equaliser

i feel this is singularly one of the greatest inventions of mankind where everyone are equal

everyone has access to an abundant source of knowledge, network, and wealth

it depends solely on you to start tapping into either or all of those

i still see a lot of arguments of abundance being a problem, but thats a load of bullshit in my opinion. abundance isn't the problem, it's weakness.

once you learn how to tap into the abundance without it affecting you negatively, the scale tips in your favour.

and the best way to tap into the abundance is to build cool things on the internet

from making content in any form -- video, audio, writing to building software, each of these give you the opportunity to tap into infinite leverage

so, start building things.
